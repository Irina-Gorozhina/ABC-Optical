About Us

At ABC Optical, we are dedicated to meeting the diverse optical needs of both children and adults. Our primary goal is to have a positive impact on the health and overall well-being of our clients. With our professional and compassionate approach, we are committed to helping individuals unlock their full potential in various aspects of life, including work, studies, sports, and leisure activities. At ABC Optical, our mission extends beyond eyewear prescriptions. We aim to empower you with the gift of clear vision, enabling you to excel in whatever you do. Whether you're pursuing a career, pursuing education, or pursuing your passions, we are here to support you on your journey. Our commitment to excellence is evident in every aspect of our service, from the moment you step into our store. As advocates of well-rounded eye care, we provide a wide range of services, including eye examinations, specialized tests, and expert advice on eyewear. Our aim is to ensure that your eyes remain healthy, vibrant, and capable of performing at their best. With ABC Optical, you're not just a patient - you are a valued member of our community. Join us in our mission to transform lives through clearer vision. Experience the difference that personalized care and dedication can make. Choose ABC Optical for your optical needs and embark on a journey of visual clarity, well-being, and endless possibilities.

Our team

Our journey begins with a strong belief in the importance of clear vision. We understand that clear sight is not just about seeing the world around us; it's about experiencing life to the fullest. Our team of passionate experts is devoted to providing personalized care and solutions that cater to the unique requirements of each individual. In a world where each person's visual needs are distinct, we take pride in offering comprehensive eye care services that go beyond traditional prescriptions. Our optometrists are dedicated to understanding the intricacies of your eyes and ensuring that you receive the best possible visual outcomes. We believe that every individual deserves the chance to experience the world with clarity and confidence.

Services

  3. Diabetic eye examinations
  4. Colour Vision assessments
  5. Macular Degeneration tests
  6. Glaucoma & eye pressure testing
  7. Checks for Cataracts

  10. Eye muscle movement and control
  11. Amblyopia (lazy eye)
  12. Contact lens fitting and management
  13. Referrals for eye laser surgery
  14. Mining & Industrial Safety Spectacles
  15. Advice on the correct fitting of good quality sunglasses (both prescription and non-prescription, in both adults and children, particularly in a sporting environment)
